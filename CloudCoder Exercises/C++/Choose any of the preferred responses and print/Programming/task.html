Introduce yourself

"Tell me a little bit about yourself." is probably the first question you probably will get asked in an interview.

http://www.indiabix.com/hr-interview/tell-me-about-yourself/

Some of the recommended responses can be the following:

  • "Good morning, sir. My name is Quick Gun Murugan."
  • "Hello Sir! My name is Quick Gun Murugan."
  • "Hello Sir! Good morning to you. My name is Quick Gun Murugan."

Choose any one of the above responses as your preference, and write a program to respond with your name, capitalized appropriately with the proper spacing between the first, middle (if any) and last name.

  • Pay attention to proper punctuations.
  • You must minimally print your full first and surname. And they cannot be "Quick" or "Gun"!
