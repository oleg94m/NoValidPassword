Teach a computer to handle introductions

Just because a computer has an IQ of 0 does not mean it should be impolite. This program will rectify the impropriety of most computers. Your program should begin by reading in a line (this will contain a name; the name will have at most 80 characters, but may include spaces). If the name is "Matthew Hertz", your program should print out "I am honored that you ran me. May I offer you 6082223.32 dollars". If any other name is entered print out "Hello, name. I am pleased to make your acquaintance." (replace name, with the line entered by the user).



Memory limit: 256 MB
Time limit: 5 seconds
